Banana Bread

A personal favorite of mine! This is a quick recipe for the classic banana bread, a sweet and sometimes nutty treat that goes great buttered and heated up! Best eaten at the workplace because getting banana bread at work is awesome.

Estimated Cook Time:35 minutes

Ingredients:

    1 egg 2 cups of granulated sugar 1 cup of cooking oil 1/2 cup of buttermilk 2 tsp. of baking soda 1 tsp. of vanilla 5 or 6 small bananas, mashed 3 cups of all-purpose flour Optional: Cup of walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and oil in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add baking soda to buttermilk and give it a stir, then mix into the bowl.
  3. Grease and flour a 9x13 or bake in small loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Serve warm with butter and enjoy!
