Sausage Cheese Balls

An incredibly fun and easy recipe sure to be the hit of any event! These bite-sized donut holes of meat and cheese fall apart in your mouth, go great with a side of ranch, and are sure to be everyones favorites in no time!

Estimate Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs. Jimmy Dean sausage.
  • 1 1/2 cups Bisquick baking mix.
  • 4 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese.
  • 1 1/2 cup of finely chopped onion.
  • 1 tsp. Accent Flavor Enhancer.

This recipe makes about 6 dozen balls. Intended for a party setting.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Form into 1 inch balls and bake 15 minutes on an un-greased cookie sheet until golden brown.
  4. Serve warm with a side of ranch.
